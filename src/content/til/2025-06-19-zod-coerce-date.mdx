title: z.coerce.date() handles YAML date strings automatically
date: 2025-06-19

YAML parses `2025-06-19` as a string, not a `Date`. Zod's `z.coerce.date()` converts it automatically — no manual `new Date()` needed. Works great in Astro content collection schemas where you want to call `.toLocaleDateString()` directly on the value.
